What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The new school canteen feels more fresh. The seats and tables have changed so that they have more space for kids to work on. The tables no longer have 4 chairs. They have 6 or more chairs to allow more kids to sit at a table to work together. This allows for more kids in a group.

  • New Attendance Style

    Our new attendance system can automatically detect if your student has arrived at school or not. If it has arrived at school, it will text you about it. If not, it will give you a warning that your child is not at school.
